Two courses. 36 holes. 14,000 yards to conquer. Weekekend athletes and seasoned pros alike thrill to the challenges of the Club’s acclaimed private courses. The Tom Fazio-designed River Course unfolds in clusters alongside the rippling Kiawah River while Tom Watson’s Cassique greens mimic the classic links of Scotland and Ireland. Both courses showcase stunning vistas as well as personalized instruction from a top-tier staff.
