Nested in the small town of Farmingdale in Monmouth County,

Eagle Oaks is everything a private golf and country club should be…a Championship caliber golf course designed by Jack Nicklaus and Johnny Miller, a stunning Southern Colonial-inspired clubhouse, and lush landscaping on 350 acres near the Shore in Monmouth County, New Jersey.

Eagle Oaks, a family-friendly club, is an ideal place for family and friends to gather and enjoy all of life’s delights. Whether a quiet dinner for two, family outdoor movie night, an afternoon round of golf with the children, or an elegant wedding or corporate function, the Club’s amenities are sure to please you.
